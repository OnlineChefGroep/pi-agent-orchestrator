Orchestration primitives that compose.

The extension stays inside the Pi host and adds the control-plane functions needed for bounded, observable multi-agent execution.

Sub-agents

Explore, Plan, Analysis, general-purpose, and custom agent definitions with inherited permissions.

Structured handoffs

Machine-readable transfers with status, evidence, decisions, and remaining work.

Swarm coordination

Dynamic join and leave behavior with live fleet status and bounded concurrency.

Scheduling

Persistent recurring jobs and a dedicated daemon schedule view.

Prompt compression

Minimal, balanced, and aggressive policies with global and per-agent controls.

TUI observability

Responsive list, resource top, schedule, performance, help, and settings views.
